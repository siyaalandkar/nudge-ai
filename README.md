What is Nudge AI?

Nudge AI is an AI-powered productivity companion that helps students 
and working professionals plan, prioritize, and complete tasks before 
deadlines are missed.

Unlike traditional reminder apps that passively ping you, Nudge AI 
uses Gemini AI to take meaningful action — breaking down assignments, 
drafting follow-up emails, generating study plans, prepping meeting 
agendas, and telling you exactly what to do next, not just that 
something is due.

---

Problem Statement

Students, professionals, and entrepreneurs frequently miss deadlines, 
assignments, meetings, bill payments, and important commitments. 
Existing tools rely on passive reminders that are easy to ignore and 
do little to help users actually complete their tasks.

Features

For Everyone
- Smart task dashboard with urgency color coding
- Real-time personalized greetings based on time of day
- AI daily action plan generated by Gemini
- Smart reminders with 3-level escalation (1 day, 3 hours, 30 mins)
- Done-for-you reminders with Gemini-drafted content inside the card
- Brain dump zone — type anything, AI organizes it into tasks
- AI deadline predictor based on task difficulty and workload
- Pomodoro focus timer with flexible session lengths
- Mood check-in that adjusts Gemini tone and recommendations
- Daily quests with XP rewards
- Achievement badge trophy shelf
- Google Drive sync for task backup and restore
- Voice input for hands-free task entry

Student Mode
- Assignment breakdown wizard — Gemini creates day-by-day subtasks
- Exam countdown wall with Gemini-generated study plans
- Study group planner — AI divides work fairly across members
- Subject-wise progress tracker with weekly charts

Professional Mode
- Auto task completion checklist generated by Gemini per task
- Meeting prep assistant — agenda and talking points 2 hours before meetings
- Work-life balance tracker with Gemini nudges when work dominates
- Email follow-up tracker with AI-drafted professional replies
