## 🌸 What is Nudge AI?

Nudge AI is an AI-powered productivity companion that helps 
students and working professionals plan, prioritize, and 
complete tasks before deadlines are missed.

Unlike traditional reminder apps that just ping you passively, 
Nudge AI uses **Gemini AI** to take meaningful action — breaking 
down assignments, drafting follow-up emails, generating study 
plans, prepping meeting agendas, and nudging you with exactly 
what to do next, not just that something is due.

## 💡 Problem Statement

Students, professionals, and entrepreneurs frequently miss 
deadlines, assignments, meetings, bill payments, and important 
commitments. Existing tools rely on passive reminders that are 
easy to ignore and do little to help users actually complete 
their tasks.

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

Nudge AI uses a **pastel doodle aesthetic** — hand-drawn SVG 
illustrations, floating animations, and a cozy digital planner 
feel designed to make productivity feel less stressful and 
more joyful.

**Color Palette:**
- 🌸 Blush Pink `#FFD6D6`
- 💜 Lavender `#E8D5FF`
- 🌿 Mint Green `#C8F0E0`
